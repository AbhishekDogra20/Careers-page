Video Editor

Job Brief:

We are looking for a talented and creative Video Editor to join our team.
As a Video Editor, you will be responsible for editing and producing compelling video content that aligns with our brand and marketing goals. Your expertise in video editing software and storytelling will help us engage our audience and elevate our video content across various platforms.

Required Skills:

1. Bachelor's degree in Film Production, Digital Media, Communications, or related field (or equivalent experience).
2. Proven experience as a Video Editor or similar role, with a strong portfolio showcasing your editing skills and creative projects.
3. Proficiency in video editing software, such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve, and familiarity with other editing tools and plugins.
4. Strong understanding of video production processes, including shooting, lighting, and audio recording techniques.
5. Excellent storytelling and narrative skills, with the ability to convey emotions, ideas, and messages effectively through video.
6. Attention to detail and ability to maintain high-quality standards while working on multiple projects simultaneously.
7. Knowledge of visual effects, motion graphics, and animation techniques is a plus.
8. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment, with strong communication and interpersonal skills.
9. Creative thinking and problem-solving skills, with a passion for experimenting with new editing techniques and styles.
10. Positive attitude, flexibility, and willingness to adapt to changing project requirements and deadlines.

Good to have skills:

1. Experience with 3D modeling and animation software, such as Blender, Cinema 4D, or Maya.
2. Knowledge of color grading techniques and software, such as Adobe After Effects or DaVinci Resolve.
3. Familiarity with video compression and encoding formats for online distribution.
4. Previous experience working in a creative agency or production studio environment.
5. Certification or training in video editing, filmmaking, or related fields.

Responsibilities

1. Collaborate with the creative team to understand project objectives, target audience, and creative direction for video content.
2. Edit raw footage into high-quality videos that tell a cohesive and engaging story, while adhering to brand guidelines and project requirements.
3. Use video editing software, such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, or DaVinci Resolve, to edit footage, add visual effects, transitions, graphics, and sound effects.
4. Enhance video quality through color correction, audio adjustments, and other post-production techniques to ensure a polished final product.
5. Incorporate music, voiceovers, and other audio elements to enhance the emotional impact and narrative flow of videos.
6. Collaborate with animators, graphic designers, and other team members to integrate visual effects, animations, and motion graphics into videos.
7. Review and provide feedback on draft versions of videos, making necessary revisions and adjustments based on feedback from stakeholders.
8. Manage video files, assets, and project timelines to ensure projects are completed on time and within budget.
9. Stay updated on industry trends, new techniques, and emerging technologies in video editing and post-production.
10. Contribute creative ideas and concepts for video content that align with brand messaging and marketing objectives.

Join our team and contribute your creativity and technical expertise to produce compelling video content that captivates our audience and brings our brand to life. Your passion for storytelling and attention to detail will play a critical role in shaping our video content strategy and driving engagement and brand awareness.
