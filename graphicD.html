Graphic Designer

Job Brief:

We are seeking a creative and talented Graphic Designer to join our team.
As a Graphic Designer, you will be responsible for conceptualizing and creating visually engaging designs across various mediums, including digital and print. You will collaborate closely with our marketing and creative teams to develop compelling visuals that effectively communicate our brand message and resonate with our target audience.

Required Skills:

1. Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Visual Arts, or related field (or equivalent experience).
2. Proven experience as a Graphic Designer or similar role, with a strong portfolio showcasing creative and professional design work.
3. Proficiency in graphic design software such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
4. Strong understanding of design principles, typography, color theory, and composition.
5. Excellent creativity and artistic ability, with a keen eye for detail and aesthetics.
6. Ability to think creatively and develop innovative design concepts that meet business objectives.
7. Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to effectively communicate ideas and concepts to team members and stakeholders.
8. Ability to work independently and manage multiple projects simultaneously, while adhering to deadlines and project requirements.
9. Experience with digital marketing platforms and understanding of web design principles is a plus.
10. Passion for design and eagerness to learn and grow in a fast-paced, creative environment.

Good to have skills:

1. Experience with motion graphics, video editing, or animation.
2. Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and web design principles.
3. Familiarity with UX/UI design principles and best practices.
4. Experience with branding and identity design.
5. Previous experience working in an agency or creative studio environment.

Responsibilities

1. Conceptualizing and designing a wide range of marketing materials, including digital ads, social media graphics, email newsletters, print collateral, and promotional materials.
2. Developing visually appealing layouts and graphics that align with brand guidelines and effectively communicate key messages.
3. Collaborating with the marketing team to brainstorm creative ideas and concepts for campaigns and projects.
4. Creating original artwork, illustrations, and icons to enhance design concepts and engage the audience.
5. Producing high-quality designs that are visually appealing, easy to understand, and optimized for various platforms and devices.
6. Managing multiple projects simultaneously and adhering to project timelines and deadlines.
7. Working closely with external vendors, printers, and other stakeholders to ensure the quality and accuracy of printed materials.
8. Keeping up-to-date with industry trends, design tools, and best practices to continuously improve skills and stay innovative.
9. Providing creative direction and feedback to junior designers and interns as needed.
10. Maintaining a strong attention to detail and ensuring all designs meet quality standards and brand guidelines.

Join our team and contribute your creative vision and design expertise to help elevate our brand and drive impactful marketing campaigns and projects.
