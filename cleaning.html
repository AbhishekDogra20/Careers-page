Cleaning Staff

Job Brief:

We are seeking diligent and reliable individuals to join our cleaning team.
As a member of our cleaning staff, you will be responsible for maintaining cleanliness and hygiene standards in our facilities. Your attention to detail and commitment to excellence will contribute to creating a safe and welcoming environment for our employees and guests.

Required Skills:

1. Previous experience in commercial or residential cleaning preferred but not required.
2. Ability to follow instructions and work independently with minimal supervision.
3. Strong attention to detail and commitment to maintaining cleanliness and hygiene standards.
4. Physical stamina and endurance to perform repetitive tasks and lift and move heavy objects as needed.
5. Good communication skills and the ability to interact professionally with coworkers, supervisors, and clients.
6. Flexibility to work various shifts, including evenings, weekends, and holidays, as needed.
7. Reliable transportation to commute to and from work locations.
8. Adherence to health and safety guidelines, including wearing appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) when necessary.
9. Positive attitude, punctuality, and willingness to learn and take on additional responsibilities as needed.
10. High school diploma or equivalent preferred.

Good to have skills:

1. Certification in cleaning and sanitation procedures or willingness to undergo training.
2. Experience using cleaning equipment and machinery safely and effectively.
3. Knowledge of environmentally friendly cleaning practices and products.
4. Ability to communicate in multiple languages to effectively serve a diverse workforce or clientele.
5. Familiarity with OSHA regulations and guidelines for workplace safety.

Responsibilities

1. Perform routine cleaning tasks, including sweeping, mopping, vacuuming, and dusting, to maintain cleanliness in designated areas such as offices, restrooms, break rooms, and common areas.
2. Clean and sanitize restrooms, including toilets, sinks, and fixtures, using appropriate cleaning agents and disinfectants.
3. Empty trash receptacles and dispose of waste in designated disposal areas.
4. Clean and disinfect surfaces, countertops, and high-touch areas to prevent the spread of germs and bacteria.
5. Replenish supplies such as toilet paper, paper towels, hand soap, and cleaning products as needed.
6. Monitor and report any maintenance issues or safety hazards to the appropriate supervisor or manager.
7. Follow established cleaning procedures and safety protocols to ensure compliance with health and safety regulations.
8. Operate cleaning equipment and machinery, such as vacuum cleaners, floor scrubbers, and pressure washers, safely and efficiently.
9. Maintain cleaning equipment and supplies in good working condition and report any maintenance or repair needs.
10. Work collaboratively with other members of the cleaning team and communicate effectively to coordinate tasks and responsibilities.

Join our team and play a vital role in maintaining a clean, safe, and healthy environment for our employees, customers, and visitors. Your hard work and dedication will contribute to the overall success and reputation of our organization.
