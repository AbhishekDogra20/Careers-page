Digital Marketer

Job Brief:

We are seeking a highly motivated and skilled Digital Marketer to join our dynamic marketing team.
As a Digital Marketer, you will be responsible for developing and implementing digital marketing strategies to drive brand awareness, engagement, and conversions across various online channels. Your creativity, analytical skills, and understanding of digital marketing tools and techniques will help us achieve our marketing goals and objectives.

Required Skills:

1. Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Business Administration, or related field (or equivalent experience).
2. Proven experience in digital marketing, with a strong track record of managing successful campaigns and driving results.
3. Expertise in digital marketing channels and tools, including social media platforms, email marketing software, Google Analytics, SEO tools, and PPC advertising platforms.
4. Excellent writing and editing skills, with the ability to create engaging and persuasive content for digital channels.
5. Strong analytical skills and ability to interpret data and metrics to make informed decisions and optimize campaign performance.
6. Creative thinking and problem-solving skills, with a passion for staying ahead of digital marketing trends and best practices.
7. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment, with strong organizational skills and attention to detail.
8. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively communicate ideas and collaborate with cross-functional teams.
9. Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and familiarity with marketing automation and CRM software.
10. Google Analytics certification and other relevant digital marketing certifications are a plus.

Good to have skills:

1. Experience with A/B testing and conversion rate optimization (CRO) techniques.
2. Knowledge of HTML/CSS and website design principles.
3. Experience with graphic design and video editing tools, such as Adobe Creative Suite.
4. Previous experience working in a B2B or B2C environment, preferably in a technology or SaaS industry.
5. Familiarity with agile marketing methodologies and project management tools.

Responsibilities

1. Develop and execute digital marketing campaigns across multiple channels, including email, social media, search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, content marketing, and display advertising.
2. Create compelling and engaging content for digital channels, including website copy, blog posts, social media posts, email newsletters, and video content.
3. Manage social media accounts and engage with followers to build brand loyalty and drive engagement.
4. Conduct keyword research and optimize website content and landing pages for SEO to improve search engine rankings and organic traffic.
5. Plan and execute PPC campaigns on platforms such as Google Ads and social media advertising to drive targeted traffic and generate leads.
6. Monitor and analyze digital marketing performance metrics, including website traffic, conversion rates, click-through rates, and social media engagement, and use data-driven insights to optimize campaigns and improve ROI.
7. Stay updated on industry trends, best practices, and emerging technologies in digital marketing and make recommendations for new strategies and tactics.
8. Collaborate with internal teams, including marketing, sales, and product development, to align digital marketing efforts with overall business objectives and priorities.
9. Manage digital marketing budgets, track expenses, and ensure campaigns are delivered on time and within budget.
10. Use marketing automation tools and CRM systems to automate marketing processes, nurture leads, and track customer interactions throughout the sales funnel.

Join our team and help us drive growth and achieve our marketing objectives through innovative digital marketing strategies and tactics. Your expertise and passion for digital marketing will play a critical role in shaping our brand's online presence and driving business success.
