Recruiter

Job Brief:

We are looking for an enthusiastic and dedicated HR Recruiter to join our team and help us attract, screen, and hire top talent.
As an HR Recruiter, you will play a crucial role in sourcing candidates, managing the recruitment process, and ensuring a positive experience for both candidates and hiring managers. You will collaborate closely with various departments to understand their staffing needs and develop effective recruitment strategies to meet organizational goals.

Required Skills:

1. Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field (or equivalent experience).
2. Proven experience as an HR Recruiter or similar role, with a strong track record of successfully filling positions across various levels and functions.
3. In-depth knowledge of recruitment techniques, sourcing strategies, and candidate assessment methods.
4. Familiarity with applicant tracking systems (ATS) and other recruitment tools and software.
5. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to build rapport with candidates and hiring managers.
6. Strong organizational skills and attention to detail, with the ability to manage multiple tasks and priorities simultaneously.
7. Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment.
8. High level of professionalism, discretion, and confidentiality.
9. Understanding of employment laws, regulations, and compliance requirements.
10. Positive attitude, proactive mindset, and willingness to learn and adapt to changing priorities and needs.

Good to have skills:

1. Professional certification in Human Resources (e.g., PHR, SHRM-CP).
2. Experience with employer branding initiatives and recruitment marketing strategies.
3. Knowledge of diversity and inclusion best practices in recruitment.
4. Previous experience working in a high-growth startup or technology company environment.
5. Familiarity with international recruitment processes and regulations.

Responsibilities

1. Collaborate with hiring managers to understand staffing needs and develop comprehensive job descriptions and recruitment plans.
2. Source and attract qualified candidates through various channels, including job boards, social media, professional networks, and referrals.
3. Screen resumes and applications to identify potential candidates who meet the job requirements and cultural fit criteria.
4. Conduct phone and in-person interviews to assess candidates' qualifications, skills, and experience.
5. Coordinate and schedule interviews with hiring managers and provide support throughout the interview process.
6. Manage the recruitment process from start to finish, including job postings, candidate sourcing, interviewing, offer negotiation, and onboarding.
7. Build and maintain a strong pipeline of candidates for current and future job openings.
8. Utilize applicant tracking systems (ATS) to manage candidate data, track recruitment metrics, and generate reports.
9. Develop and maintain relationships with external recruiting agencies, job boards, and other talent acquisition partners.
10. Stay updated on industry trends, best practices, and legal requirements related to recruitment and employment.

Join our team and play a key role in building a talented and diverse workforce that drives our organization's success and growth.
