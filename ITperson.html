IT Specialist

Job Brief:

We are looking for a proactive and skilled IT Specialist to join our team.
As an IT Specialist, you will play a crucial role in maintaining our organization's IT infrastructure, providing technical support to employees, and ensuring the smooth operation of our systems and networks. Your expertise will help us optimize technology resources, enhance cybersecurity measures, and support our team in achieving their goals.

Required Skills:

1. Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field (or equivalent experience).
2. Proven experience as an IT Specialist, Systems Administrator, or similar role, with a strong technical background in IT infrastructure management and support.
3. Proficiency in troubleshooting hardware, software, and network issues, with hands-on experience in Windows and/or Linux environments.
4. Familiarity with network protocols, TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, VPN, and other networking concepts.
5. Experience with virtualization technologies, such as VMware or Hyper-V, and cloud services, such as AWS or Azure, is a plus.
6. Knowledge of cybersecurity principles, best practices, and compliance standards, such as GDPR and HIPAA.
7. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to diagnose and resolve technical issues efficiently.
8. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical users.
9. Ability to work independently and prioritize tasks effectively in a fast-paced environment.
10. Certification in relevant IT disciplines, such as CompTIA A+, Network+, Security+, or Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate (MCSA), is preferred.

Good to have skills:

1. Experience with ITIL framework and IT service management processes.
2. Knowledge of scripting languages, such as PowerShell for automation and scripting tasks.
3. Familiarity with enterprise-level IT management tools, such as Active Directory, Group Policy, and system monitoring solutions.
4. Previous experience working in a regulated industry, such as healthcare or finance, with knowledge of industry-specific compliance requirements.
5. Project management skills and experience leading IT projects from initiation to completion.

Responsibilities

1. Provide technical support and troubleshooting assistance to employees for hardware, software, and network-related issues.
2. Install, configure, and maintain hardware and software components, including desktops, laptops, printers, servers, operating systems, and productivity applications.
3. Set up and configure network equipment, including routers, switches, firewalls, and wireless access points, to ensure reliable connectivity and performance.
4. Monitor system performance, network traffic, and security logs to identify and address potential issues proactively.
5. Perform regular maintenance tasks, such as software updates, patches, backups, and system optimizations, to ensure optimal performance and reliability.
6. Manage user accounts, permissions, and access rights in accordance with security policies and best practices.
7. Implement and enforce cybersecurity measures, including antivirus software, firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and data encryption, to protect against cyber threats and unauthorized access.
8. Develop and maintain documentation, including system configurations, network diagrams, troubleshooting procedures, and user guides, to facilitate knowledge sharing and training.
9. Collaborate with external vendors and service providers to troubleshoot complex issues, procure hardware and software, and implement new technologies.
10. Stay updated on emerging technologies, industry trends, and best practices in IT management and cybersecurity to continuously improve skills and knowledge.

Join our team and be part of a collaborative and innovative environment where your technical expertise will contribute to the success and growth of our organization.
