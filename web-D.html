Web Developer

Job Brief:

We are seeking a talented and passionate Web Developer to join our dynamic team.
As a Web Developer, you will be responsible for designing, coding, and modifying websites, from layout to function, according to our specifications. You will strive to create visually appealing sites that feature user-friendly design and clear navigation.

Required Skills:

1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, or related field (or equivalent experience).
2. Proven experience as a Web Developer or similar role, with a strong portfolio showcasing previous work.
3. Proficiency in front-end development languages and frameworks, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and jQuery.
4. Experience with responsive web design and development techniques, including CSS frameworks like Bootstrap or Tailwind.
5. Familiarity with back-end development technologies, such as PHP, Python, or Ruby on Rails, and understanding of MVC frameworks.
6. Experience with content management systems (CMS) such as WordPress, Joomla, or Drupal.
7. Strong understanding of web standards, accessibility, and cross-browser compatibility issues.
8. Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
9. Effective communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work well in a team environment.
10. Ability to manage multiple projects and prioritize tasks effectively.

Good to have skills:

1. Experience with version control systems such as Git.
2. Knowledge of UX/UI principles and design tools like Adobe Creative Suite.
3. Understanding of server-side programming languages and frameworks.
4. Familiarity with database management systems such as MySQL or MongoDB.
5. Experience with e-commerce platforms and payment gateway integration.

Responsibilities:

1. Designing and implementing websites from initial concept, site architecture, and user interface to finished deliverable.
2. Creating visually appealing site layouts and user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
3. Collaborating with designers and back-end developers to ensure seamless integration of front-end and back-end functionality.
4. Developing and maintaining responsive web design principles to ensure that websites are optimized for various devices and screen sizes.
5. Implementing best practices in web development, including accessibility standards, SEO principles, and performance optimization techniques.
6. Conducting thorough testing and debugging of websites to ensure cross-browser compatibility, responsiveness, and functionality.
7. Staying updated on emerging technologies and industry trends to continuously improve skills and knowledge.
8. Troubleshooting and resolving website issues as they arise, including identifying and addressing security vulnerabilities.
9. Working closely with other team members and stakeholders to understand project requirements and provide timely updates on progress.
10. Contributing to the ongoing enhancement of our web development processes and standards.

Join our team and be part of a collaborative environment where your creativity and expertise will contribute to the success of exciting web projects.
